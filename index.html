Gym Equipment

Treadmill

Used to perform cardiovascular workouts such as jog, run, or walk in the same place.

Preacher Bench

Provides controlled environment for developing and strengthening biceps

Pull Up Bar

Popular for building upper body strength, targeting the muscles in the back, shoulders, arms, and core


Lat Pull Down

Allows users to perform a pulling motion similar to that of a pull-up or chin-up. Targeting muscles in the upper back and shoulders.

Spin Bike

Spin biking provides an effective cardiovascular workout, improving heart health and endurance. Engages several muscle groups and promotes calorie burning.

Leg Extension

Used for strength training exercise that primarily targets the quadriceps muscles in the front of the thighs.
